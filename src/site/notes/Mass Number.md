# Mass Number
Refers to the sum of the number of neutrons and protons in any atom nucleus. It is called "Mass" number since most of the mass of any atom rests in the nucleus of the atom.

It is widely used in Chemistry and Particle Physics. Ocasionally appearing in Nuclear Physics when discussing [[Nuclear Fission Reactions\|Nuclear Fission Reactions]]
