# Mohammad Askari Mirza
> The third son of [[Babur\|Babur]] and sibling of [[Kamran Mirza\|Kamran Mirza]] (both born from the same mother) while being the half-brother of [[Humayun\|Humayun]] - the eldest son of Babur and 2nd ruler of the [[Mughal Empire\|Mughal Empire]].

Humayun became the Emperor and Kamran was handed the reigns of Kabul and Kandahar, Mohammad Askari Mirza was handed the governorship of the city of Sambhal as a part of the last wish of Bābur.
