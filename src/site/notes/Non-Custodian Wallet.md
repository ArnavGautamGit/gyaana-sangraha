# Non-Custodian Wallet (NC Wallet)
> It is a type of Digital Wallet used to store [[Cryptocurrency\|Cryptocurrency]] where the keys to that Wallet remain in the hands of the users. It is the opposite of a [[Custodian Wallet\|Custodian Wallet]].

This is great because although you have to manage your keys and seed phrases yourselves, Non-Custodian Wallets cannot be stolen from unless the hackers know your seed phrases (which are used to generate your keys).

The most well-known and well respected name in the business is Metamask for a long time ever since 2021 and 2022. New ones like Cake Wallet have entered the game much later but have been just as popular.

### How to Fund them?
You fund them with mined [[Cryptocurrency\|Cryptocurrency]] for maximum privacy or you can get paid from another NC Wallet. Other methods of buying crypto in any Crypto Exchange and then transferring funds here is also a way to go but most of them would demand KYC & hence hurt your [[Personal Online Privacy\|Personal Privacy]].
