# Bhutan
> [[State (United Nations)\|UN Member State]] at the border of [[India\|India]] and Tibet ([[China\|China]] since 1959). The country is well-known for being one of the happiest countries in the world.

They are currently builfing the [[Gelephu Mindfulness City\|Gelephu Mindfulness City]] in Bhutan which will be a sustainable city for economic development in the region. Bhutan also became the battleground for the [[Indo-Sino Dokalam Conflict (2017)\|Indo-Sino Dokalam Conflict (2017)]].

India operates and builds many Hydropower projects in Bhutan where excess/surplus power is then shared with India. India is a powerful ally for Bhutan against [[China\|China]].

### Demography
The population of the Kingdom of Bhutan are 75% Buddhist, 24% Hindu and around 1% of the people follow other faiths such as [[Islam\|Islam]].
