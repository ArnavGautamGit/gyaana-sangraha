# Decentralised Network
> Also known as "Decentralised Technology", it is a digital product or service that is not owned by a single company, not hosted from a single server nor controlled by a single device, person or corporate.  

Decentralised Networks and Technologies on a smaller scale may only fulfill one of the above mentioned criteria (like Decentralised [[Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs)\|UAVs]]) and in other cases, decentralisation maybe a small part of an overall much bigger technology like [[Blockchain\|Blockchain]] technology that was first brought to the world's notice with the launch of now-famous [[Cryptocurrency\|Cryptocurrency]] known as [[Bitcoin (BTC)\|Bitcoin (BTC)]].
