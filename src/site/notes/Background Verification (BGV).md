# Background Verification (BGV)
> Recruiting Practice performed mostly by HR or a secondary company that specialises in this department. 

BGV often only includes your new company's HR to call up the previous company's HRs and asks about you and your performance. Note that "you" here refers to the interview candidate. The questions generally ential questions such as verifyinf whether you were employed there and whether you were a good employee in terms of performance and behaviour, your habits, way of working among other things like if you have indulged in anything illegal or immoral while at the previous company.

Sometimes (although rarely) companies even check your public posts on social media whether you have posted something or not. If yes, then what all have you posted about earlier. Do you engage in heated debates and did you ever behave un-professionally online.
