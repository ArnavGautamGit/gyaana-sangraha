# Owlesia
> An old Empire in Lurov, now a part of the [[Kingdom of Dragonpeaks\|Kingdom of Dragonpeaks]]. Located in the ease of the Mountain ranges & north of [[Magical Woods\|Magical Woods]].

Current Duke: [[Sir Arthur I Nightingale\|Sir Arthur I Nightingale]]

It was an estate which was formerly ruled by the [[House Forechester\|House Forechester]] while the [[House Raebershire\|Raebershires]] were the Counts and Barons but upgraded to Dukes since the Forechesters themselves would rule all of the merged lands of both Owlesia and Dragonpeaks after [[The Wedding of Peace\|The Wedding of Peace]].
It was here [[House Nightingale\|House Nightingale]] were handed baronny over the cities of Triton, Seashire and surrounding towns.

After [[The Raebershire Rebellion\|The Raebershire Rebellion]], when they were ousted they were replaced by the Nightingales since Sir Arthur played a big role in saving the life of [[John VI/I Forechester\|King John VI/I]].
