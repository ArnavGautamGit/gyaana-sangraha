# Vulnerable (DH)
> A Condition or State in [[Compendiums/Daggerheart Compendium/Daggerheart\|Daggerheart]] where any creature is vulnerable to being hit i.e., they have disadvantage on roles for attacking/defending but others have advantage on rolls against them.

This condition, similar to [[Restrained (DH Condition)\|Restrained]] and [[Hidden (DH Condition)\|Hidden]] can affect any character whether it is an NPC or it is a Player Character.
