# Avoid your department being Thrown Under The Bus
> Sometimes someone else may try to throw an employee in your department under the boss by hitting reply all with a CC to your Boss...

Follow this guide to prevent your entire department being thrown under the bus with their nose in the mud. We use the "chain of command" check.

> [!cite] Hit Reply all with this email underneath
> Hey {name}, I notice you CC'ed the executives on a minor operational fix. In the future please bring those coaching moments directly to me so that we are not cluttering anyone's inboxes.

You publically called him out for their petty behaviour while maintaining your professionalism perfectly. This email has minimal passive-aggressive undertones especially with using "please" to appear more polite.
