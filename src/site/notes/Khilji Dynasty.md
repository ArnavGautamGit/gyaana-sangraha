# Khilji Dynasty
> Also spelled as "Khalji", it is the name of the second dynasty to rule the [[Delhi Sultanate\|Delhi Sultanate]] after the instability faced by succession to [[Ghiyas ud-Din Balban\|Ghiyas ud-Din Balban]] of the [[Mamluk Dynasty (Delhi)\|Qutubi Mamluks]].

Khilji or Khalji were the most degenerate and devastating dynasty for Indian Culture.
It began with the defeat of [[Muizzuddin Qaiqabad\|Muizzuddin Qaiqabad]] of the [[Mamluk Dynasty (Delhi)\|Mamluk Dynasty]] at the hands of [[Jalaluddin Khilji\|Jalaluddin Khilji]].

Jalaluddin became the new [[Delhi Sultanate\|Sultān of Delhi]] and began his own lineage since he was not of pure [[Turkic People\|Turkic]] blood, Khilji was part Afghani blood and hence began his own dynasty (or historians refer to it as a separate dynasty for this reason idk)

### Sequence of Sultāns
[[Jalaluddin Khilji\|Jalaluddin Khilji]] ---> 1290 AD to 1296 AD
[[Alauddin Khilji\|Alauddin Khilji]] ---> 1296 AD to 1316 AD
[[Mallik Kafur\|Mallik Kafur]] ---> 1316 AD (for a few months as regent for [[Shihab ud-Din Omar\|Shihab ud-Din Omar]])
[[Mubarak Shah Khilji\|Mubarak Shah Khilji]] ---> 1316 AD to 1320 AD

### Origins of the Khalji/Khilji tribe of warriors
It is widely believed and to a great extent proven that the Khilji surname comes from the [[Khalaj Tribe\|Khalaj Tribe]] in Iran who are mostly persianised now. They most likely converted to Islam during the [[Islamisation of Persia\|Islamisation of Persia]]
