# Twitter is Cyanide for Privacy
> Most people have a Twitter account lying around even despite their changes in Privacy Policies and more importantly their change in leadership and eventual control by Musk.

Twitter over time has accumulated a lot of information about your interests, political leanings and if you have ever purchased a premium/verified blue tick then they also have your payment information. This is the absolute worst.

Moreover, it may also have your photo, email, possibly even your phone number (unless you use [[VoIP Numbers\|VoIP Numbers]] and [[Burner Emails\|Burner Emails]]) that could leak in a data breach (especially with someone as non-chalant as Musk in control of the site now).

Tweets from your Twitter accounts must be wiped before outright account deletion since they have a great amount of data on you about political beliefs and other things such as who you tag & mention often, your network map of people you know and now it publically shows all that data apart from showing your location to everyone on the planet. They can see your country where your account is based which is not very nice for privacy (because it means they have your location at account creation).

Since right below it the app also shows your method of connection (the Google Play Store or Apple App Store servers of a specific country) then it is officially broadcasting your Operating System apart from just storing that info. They do not give a rat's ass about privacy so you should not give a rat's ass about their app either.
