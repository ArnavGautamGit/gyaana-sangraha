# Storms of Eslance
> HOOK: A Ship carrying diplomats to promote peace & trade between two kingdoms is found crashed and burnt on an island. Mercenaries are sent to investigate.

System used: [[Compendiums/Daggerheart Compendium/Daggerheart\|Daggerheart]], [[Compendiums/D&D Compendium/Dungeons & Dragons\|D&D 5E (2024)]]
Setting: [[Lurov\|Lurov]] 
Price: $15/session for each player 
Joining Link: [StartPlaying Games]()

Themes: Espionage, Political Intrigue, Fantasy, High-Fantasy.
Style: a mixture of "Theatre of the Mind" & "Owlbear Rodeo" VTT.

### Game Description
Gear up to go on an adventurous journey to prevent a war that may engulf & destroy the entire world as you travel the world as mercenaries for hire.
Will you save your kingdom and the world or will your actions tip the scales towards ruin?
No previous experience in Daggerheart/D&D 5E required.
