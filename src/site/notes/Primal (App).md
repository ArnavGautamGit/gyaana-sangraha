# Primal (App)
> App Client on [[NOSTR\|NOSTR]] which creatres a social media similar to twitter but also has articles and things of that nature. Since it is on Nostr, it is censorship-resistant.

It is one of the [[NOSTR\|NOSTR]] services where I have an account. I will not reveal the account for reasons relating to my [[Personal Online Privacy\|Personal Online Privacy]] but I am there.

### Concerns of Custodial Wallets
Here, just like on many [[NOSTR\|NOSTR]] Clients, [[Satoshi (SAT)\|Satoshis]] are used for Zap payments among other things such as peer to peer transfer. Although you can put your own Satoshi Wallet Address for people to directly send you Zaps, to Zap to other people, you have to transfer your Sats from your personal [[Non-Custodian Wallet\|Non-Custodial Wallet]] to an in-app Primal [[Custodian Wallet\|Custodial Wallet]]. Which I find a little weird. I do not keep more than 50 sats in that custodial wallet at any time.

Note that anytime people click the Zap button on your post (called Note on Primal) you get paid in your in-app Custodial Wallet but if they go to your profile and use the link to your wallet from there, then that's the only way to get the money to your external wallet which I always recommend should be a [[Non-Custodian Wallet\|Non-Custodial Wallet]].

### Small Community
Primal and [[NOSTR\|NOSTR]] in general is such a small community right now being under 2 years old in 2026 (since it started in 2024) and unlike [[Bluesky\|Bluesky]], it is not a politically-driven platform. This means that the total active users are less enough that you do not have any followers.

The small little community that you do get on here is not driven by any algorithm to suggest stuff to you, it is very natural and similar to early internet - party of this is the fact that the feed may not show you something new everyday unless you follow a spammer or something. This may or may not be a good thing depending upon your opinion of [[Digital Minimalism\|Digital Minimalism]] and whether you consider it a good thing or not.

Due to a lack of a suggestion algorithm, building a community and providing value may work for some and may not for others because visibility is sheerly based on how many people have the same interests as you. Once you get to around 1000 to 2000 followers however things seem to be getting easier in terms of builfing a community there.

### Limited Revenue Potential (from Conventional Revenue Streams)
Most Social Media makes money with ads being served which are priced on the basis of total number of unique persons using your products.

Since [[NOSTR\|NOSTR]] is a [[Decentralised Network\|Decentralised Technology]] that forms the backbone protocol of all the Clients (like Primal) that are built on it, there is no way of estimating the exact number of users on it especially being a small community that relies entirely on [[Asymmetric Key Encryption\|Public Key Cryptography]]based account creation using Public and Private Keys, there is nothing stopping users from using multiple public keys to have multiple accounts on Nostr & hence having multiple accounts.

This can hurt the revenue potential either way since the only way to make estimates more accurate is to have more data collection while more data collection will be hated so much by the community since the promise to come to Nostr was [[Personal Online Privacy\|Personal Privacy]] and no identifiers unless you want to be identified by your name.

For this reason, Primal have started their premium tiers above the regular free app but there are no real limitations as such of the free version of the app that anyone can use.
