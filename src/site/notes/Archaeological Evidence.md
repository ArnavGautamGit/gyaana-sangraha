# Archaeological Evidence
> Using the Material Remains of past human activity to prove a theory, understand a culture or reconstruct the [[History\|History]] of a group of people among other usecases where it can be referenced.

Archaelogical Evidence usually includes other fields of Archaelogical Study like [[Epigraphy\|Epigraphy]] (Study of Inscriptions) and [[Numismatics\|Numismatics]] (Study of Coins) along with any [[Artefacts\|Artefacts]] such as [[Pottery\|Pottery]]. Often times [[Historical Architecture\|Historical Architecture]] contains inscriptions or themselves can be used to identify the histories of a people - their distinct style acting as evidence of lineage or cultural exchange between two communities.
