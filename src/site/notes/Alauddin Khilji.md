# Alauddin Khilji
> Second Ruler of [[Khilji Dynasty\|Khilji Dynasty]] and the 12th [[Delhi Sultanate\|Sultān of Delhi]] known for mistreating Hindus and their kingdoms.

![[Alauddin Khilji.png\|Alauddin Khilji.png]]

He was the most infamous of the Khiljis and what caused Hindus to abhorrently hate the Khiljis and their Dynasty forever. Although he did introduce Market Reforms and Agrarian Reforms

### Mallik Kafur & Military Campiagns
He found that a military general and strategist is currently in the Afghan slave market on sale who will be of service as a [[Mamluk\|Mamluk]] of the purchaser. Alauddin purchased [[Mallik Kafur\|Mallik Kafur]] for 1000 Dinar Gold Coins.

[[Mallik Kafur\|Mallik Kafur]] led many invasions on the behalf of his master.
Mallik Kafur accompanied Khilji on the [[Siege of Ranstambhapura (1301)\|Siege of Ranstambhapura (1301)]] and other war campaigns in the Rajputana territories until the [[Siege of Jalor (1311)\|Siege of Jalor (1311)]].

Mallik Kafur failed the first siege of Ranstambhapura (Ranthambore) in 1301 but then succeeded on the second siege in 1302. He beseiged [[Chittorgarh\|Chittorgarh]] with [[Amir Khusrau\|Amir Khusrau]] and according to a few Hindu sources, Khilji himself was present on the siege. 

We can understand their mindset as Hinduphobic that while they attacked Hindu kingdoms in North and Central India, he never attacked [[Nasiruddin Bughra Khan\|Nasiruddin Bughra Khan]] and the [[Bengal Sultanate\|Bengal Sultanate]] because apparently taking out [[Kaafir\|Kaafir]] Hindus was a bigger priority.

[[Mallik Kafur\|Mallik Kafur]] was then sent on his independent campaigns where he had full strategic autonomy. Kafur defeated 4 major kingdoms in these campaigns. 
1. [[Sultanate Invasion of Devagiri\|Sultanate Invasion of Devagiri]] against the [[Yadavas of Devagiri\|Yadavas of Devagiri]]
2. [[Sultanate Invasion of Warangal\|Sultanate Invasion of Warangal]] against the [[Kakatiyas of Warangal\|Kakatiyas of Warangal]]
3. [[Sultanate Invasion of Dwasamudra\|Sultanate Invasion of Dwasamudra]] against the [[Hoysala Kingdom of Dwasamudra\|Hoysala Kingdom of Dwasamudra]]
4. [[Sultanate Invasion of Madurai\|Sultanate Invasion of Madurai]] against the [[Later Pandya Dynasty\|Later Pandya Dynasty]]

Mallik Kafur won all the territories and the muslim soldiers raped, pillaged and sacked the cities and temples before returning (unless stated otherwise in the isolated instances listed in the exact notes above). He only established formal control but there was never an interest from the Sultanate to establish permanent establishment here since it is of no use.

### Agrarian Reforms and Market Control Mechanisms
Alauddin Khilji introduced market control by setting a price ceiling on 3 essential commodities: Food, Horses and Slaves. I guess the last one is truly essential for a degenerate empire.

To make sure that these price ceilings were respected he deployed spies named Barid & Muhiyan who would crosscheck & surveil to make sure no trader was selling at a price above the ceiling.

He divided all land into two categories that is Ibta & Khālis. All the produce from the Khālis land will be used for the [[Delhi Sultanate\|Delhi Sultanate]] and the Sultān to maintain armies and cavalry. Entire Ganga-Yamuna Doab was declared as Khālis under Alauddin.

In non-Khālis land, the tax was blanket tax of 50% of all produce. Ropes were used to measure land & the size of land was mentioned in land grant documents.

### Military Reforms
Time to Time Facial & Body Inspection to maintain readiness called as "Chehra" and "Huliya" meaning face & physical state respectively. 

Paper Documents of Horse Health were maintained with data of dated medical history of the horse. Horses or their equipment was stamped with a seal after each visit. This procedure was called "Dāgh"

### Death & Aftermath
Alauddin Khilji died in 1316 AD. His toddler son [[Shihab ud-Din Omar\|Shihab ud-Din Omar]] became the next i.e., the 13th [[Delhi Sultanate\|Sultān of Delhi]] and 3rd of this dynasty. [[Mallik Kafur\|Mallik Kafur]] ruled as the regent Nāib Sultān. The Market control mechanisms went to the dogs & even Shihab ud-Din & Kafur were replaced after just 1 month by Alauddin's elder son [[Mubarak Shah Khilji\|Mubarak Shah Khilji]].
