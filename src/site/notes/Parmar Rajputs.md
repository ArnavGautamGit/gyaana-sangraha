# Parmar Rajputs
> Also spelled as "Paramāra" and "Parmaar"
> Parmar (परमार) were among one of the many Rajput Clans that gained prominence AFTER the [[Pratihara Empire\|Pratihara Empire]] lost the ruler [[Mihira Bhoja\|Mihira Bhoja]] and started to become too complacent in power.

Parmars were not limited to Malwa, although their Malwa line is most famous & was at its zenith during the rule of [[King Bhoja\|King Bhoja]] in 1040s till the death of Bhoja himself. They eventually became one of the biggest clans of the [[Rajputana Confederacy\|Rajputana Confederacy]] by military strength alongside the [[Chauhan Rajputs\|Chauhan Rajput Clan]] by the time of the [[First Battle of Tarain (1191)\|First Battle of Tarain (1191)]].
