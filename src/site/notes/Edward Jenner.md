# Edward Jenner
> Revolutionary Man who kicked off a revolution in healthcare in 1749 with his creation of the [[Smallpox Vaccine\|Smallpox Vaccine]] to fight the disease of [[Smallpox\|Smallpox]].

Smallpox was one of the largest killers in recent European history at the time of the Jenner. While Europeans could not be affected by Smallpox after mass-vaccination built immunity but it does not mean that they could not become carriers of the disease.
