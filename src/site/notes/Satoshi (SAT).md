# Satoshi (SAT)
> Satoshi is the smallest unit of [[Bitcoin (BTC)\|Bitcoin (BTC)]] named after Satoshi Nakamoto - the only piece of confirmed information about the man who founded of Bitcoin is his name.

Satoshi is the smallest indivisible unit of the original [[Bitcoin (BTC)\|Bitcoin]] which operates a separate chain known as the [[Lightning Network (LN)\|Lightning Network (LN)]]. Making BTC the only [[Cryptocurrency\|Crypto]] to have more than one chains where it operates.
$$\Large\text{1 Bitcoin} = \text{100 Million Satoshis}$$
or in other words, we can also say that:
$$\Large 1\ \text{Satoshi} = 0.00000001\ \text{Bitcoin}$$

### Ordinal Numbers
One can mine Bitcoin on either the main chain (the old chain since 2009) or Layer 2 chain also known as the [[Lightning Network (LN)\|Lightning Network (LN)]]. When mined from LN chain, the Satoshis have a unique number reflective of the order in which it was mined, it is called an Ordinal Number.

### Bitcoin Ordinal (also called Bitcoin NFTs but dummies)
Bitcoin Ordinal is another term which is shortened to Ordinal which refers to a specific kind of sat which stores a piece of text or a picture on it. It is meant to be traded as a digital asset similar to a [[Non-Fungible Token (NFT)\|Non-Fungible Token (NFT)]] but it is even more different. It is so less known that at the current point of time, people might get scammed with them.

### Use for Zaps in Nostr
Zaps are a way of payment on [[NOSTR\|NOSTR]] which may be used to pay for services like [[Satlantis\|Satlantis]], maybe used for paying as a form of endorsment on Social Media Clients on the NOSTR Protocol like [[Primal (App)\|Primal (App)]].

### Never use Sats for Privacy
They are still on the BTC chain and a [[Public Ledger\|Public Ledger]] just like old Layer-1. For Privacy, use either [[Monero (XMR)\|Monero (XMR)]] or [[ZCash (ZEC)\|ZCash (ZEC)]].
