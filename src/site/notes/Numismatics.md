# Numismatics
> The Study of minted, stamped or other kinds of Coins that existed throughout [[History\|History]] to understand the local culture and/or reconstruct the History from a particular time period.

Numismatics are one of the forms of [[Archaeological Evidence\|Archaeological Record]] that focuses primarily on being able to reconstruct the financial situation of the times being studied.
