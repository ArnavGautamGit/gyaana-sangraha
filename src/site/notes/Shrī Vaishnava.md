# Shrī Vaishnava
> A Tradition among the [[Vaishnava Tradition of Hinduism\|Vaishnava Tradition of Hinduism]] which is one of the four major roots of the Vaishnava Tradition.

It is the parent root of [[Shrī Rāmānuja Sampradāya\|Shrī Rāmānuja Sampradāya]] and [[Shrī Rāmānanda Sampradāya\|Shrī Rāmānanda Sampradāya]] in chronological order as the latter emerged in the 14th Century.
Both of them have their own scriptures that they consider supreme.
