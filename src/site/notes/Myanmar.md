# Myanmar
> [[State (United Nations)\|UN Member State]] bordering Thailand, [[India\|India]] and [[China\|China]]. The country is the western-most country that is a part of the [[Association of South East Asian Nations (ASEAN)\|Association of South East Asian Nations (ASEAN)]].

Myanmar is one of the most important nations for [[India\|India]]'s connection to ASEAN and maintaining peace & stability in the North East region of India - especially with the respect to Myanmari-origin [[Kuki Tribe\|Kuki Tribe]] involved in the [[Manipur Meitei-Kuki Conflict\|Manipur Meitei-Kuki Conflict]].

Myanmar is currently under a [[Myanmar's Military Coup\|Military Coup]] since 2021 which has given rise to [[Arakan Army\|Arakan Army]] near the border of [[Bangladesh\|Bangladesh]].

Other projects of Myanmar include:
[[India-Myanmar-Thailand Trilateral Highway Project\|India-Myanmar-Thailand Trilateral Highway Project]]
[[China Myanmar Economic Corridor (CMEC)\|China Myanmar Economic Corridor (CMEC)]]
[[India-Myanmar Kaladan Project\|India-Myanmar Kaladan Project]]
