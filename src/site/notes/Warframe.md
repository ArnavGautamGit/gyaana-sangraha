# Warframe: Everything about the 2013 game!
> Warframe is an [[MMORPG\|MMORPG]] that came out in 2013 and is still going strong with a growing community and lore around it. It derives its name after the Warframes - the main suited characters controlled by the player (YOU).

To understand this guide you may need to learn about the [[Background Lore of Warframe\|Background Lore of Warframe]] to understand the factions and their characteristics. It allows to understand why certain damage types work better against certain factions. This Background Lore updates you about how the Universe comes to be. I will not spoil the Second Dream. No matter what. You will have to play the Second Dream to know what the Operator Mode is.
