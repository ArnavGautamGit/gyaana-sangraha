# Nidus I Bronze
> A Bronze-scaled [[Compendiums/Daggerheart Compendium/Player/Ancestries/Drakona (DH)\|Drakona (DH)]] of [[House Bronze\|House Bronze]]. He also serves as the Duke of [[Castle Whitebreak\|Whitebreak]] and the Mountain Quaries by the lineage of his father who was also the Duke. After his father passed in [[The Raebershire Rebellion\|The Raebershire Rebellion]], his squire and friend helps him run Whitebreak as the Captain of Whitebreak Command - [[Charles Ravenshield\|Charles Ravenshield]].

He can be spotted by his distinct White Mohawk of hair and wearing his coat of arms on his surcoat and shield. A Gules (Red) shield charged with three Dragon Heads all cabossed Or (Gold).
