# Fighter (D&D)
> Masters of the Weapons & Armour - there is not a single weapon they cannot use.
> Questing knights, royal champions, elite soldiers, and hardened mercenaries - as Fighters, they all share an unparalleled prowess with weapons and armor. And they are well acquainted with death, both meting it out and defying it.

You will be choosing a fighting style at Level 1 and it is recommended that "Defence" is the one that is chosen. The details of the Fighting Styles is at the bottom of the table.

### Fighting Style
At Level 1, you choose a Fighting Style at "Defence" is recommended to avoid relying on Second Wind since you are limited by how many times you can use it. 
The other options include War Caster, Archery, Blindfigthing, Dueling, Great Weapon Fighting among others. Have a look at them at Page 205 in the [[Compendiums/D&D Compendium/Corebooks/D&D Player's Handbook\|D&D Player's Handbook]] to take a pick among the given options where each one gives you a different Class Feature and Benefits.

### Subclasses
Each Character needs to pick their subclass at Level 3
- Battle Master
- Champion
- Eldritch Knight
- Psi Warrior
