# Graha (ग्रह) in Kundali
> [[Graha (ग्रह)\|Graha (ग्रह)]] rule over Rāshi. Except [[Sūrya (सूर्य)\|Sūrya (सूर्य)]] and [[Chandra (चंद्र)\|Chandra (चंद्र)]], all the Graha rule 2 Rāshis. The rāshi which they govern are called Svarāshi, the rāshi in which they are their strongest (exaulted) are Uccharāshi. The rāshi in which they are the weakest is their nīcharāshi (or neecharāshi). All Grahas aspect the 7th house from their position apart from a few other houses.
