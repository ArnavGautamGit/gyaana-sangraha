# Silence the Condescending Coworker
> A coworker who is constantly condescending smart know-it-all who looks down at you and beats you down for not doing good enough or knowing well enough about a topic can lead to you having a needlessly testing and hectic time at your workplace (even when all is going well)

It is important to deal with such people who suck out the Life and energy out of your workday and prevent you from being productive. Let's see how with this guide.

### Level 1: Explain it Trap
Because this smart know-it-all believes that they are smarter than you are but also loves to flaunt it. Use it against them. When they make a passive-aggressive condescending comment, please do not reply emotionally. Just play dumb.

> [!cite] Trap them in their own explanation
> "I am unsure whether I understood the tone behind your comment. Could you please break down exactly what you mean by it?"

Force them to explain their disrespect out loud so everyone knows how much of a disrespectful condescending thing.

### Level 2: Ask them for the Source
If they make a comment such as "the only people that cannot do that are too dumb to be employed in such an esteemed company" ask them to provide the dataset. 

Say "Could you please provide the dataset so I can crosscheck the information?"
99.999999999999999999% of the time (unless it is truly a one-sided bogus study they can find) these condescending shits never have the data to back their claims.

### Level 3: Formally show them their place
Tell them that you only follow the advise and opinions of your boss and as long as they do not get promoted they can take their opinions elsewhere since you have no fucks to give.

> [!cite] Professional Dismissal of their Theories
> "I appreciate your input but I am alligning with the directives from my manager/boss for this one. I will let you know if we need to pivot to your theories. Until then, please do not interfere in my line of work."
