# Jalaluddin Khilji
> Founder and First Sultān of the [[Khilji Dynasty\|Khilji Dynasty]] ruling the [[Delhi Sultanate\|Delhi Sultanate]]

![[Jalaluddin Khilji.png\|Jalaluddin Khilji.png]]

He was one of the Khiljis who barely got time to commit cruelties while his successive kings would do much more damage to the Hindu civilisation of India.

### Assassination & Succession
To snatch away the throne from his father-in-law, [[Alauddin Khilji\|Alauddin Khilji]] assassinated Jalaluddin in 1296 AD while [[Rukn ud-Din Ibrahim\|Rukn ud-Din Ibrahim]] ruled as regent due to Jalal's worsening health. As soon as the assassination occured, the Throne went to the monster named Alauddin who was insanely cruel to all Hindus as he saw them as [[Kaafir\|Kaafir]].
