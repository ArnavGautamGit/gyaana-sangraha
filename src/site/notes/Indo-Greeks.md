# Indo-Greeks
> Ethinicity of people from North-west India (modern Pakistan & Afghanistan) who have mixed ethnicity of Indian & Greek after the Greeks from the [[Selucid Empire\|Selucid Empire]] signed [[The Treaty of The Indus (303 BC)\|Mauryan-Selucid Treaty of 303 BC]].

The Indo-Greeks of this region broke away from the [[Mauryan Empire\|Mauryan Empire]] after Brihadratha Maurya
was assassinated to end the Mauryan rule & [[Shunga Dynasty\|Shunga Dynasty]] began. This kingdom was formed by [[Menander I Soter\|Menander I Soter]] and known as [[Yavana Confederacy\|Yavana Confederacy]].

[[Menander I Soter\|Menander I Soter]] later converted to [[Bauddha (Buddhism)\|Buddhism]] & this conversion began the trend of more and more Indo-Greeks converting to Buddhism slowly becoming [[Indo-Greek Buddhists\|Indo-Greek Buddhists]] later. These Indo-Greeks got coverted and Islamised later in the [[Islamisation of G훮ndh훮ra (Afghanistan)\|Islamisation of G훮ndh훮ra (Afghanistan)]].
