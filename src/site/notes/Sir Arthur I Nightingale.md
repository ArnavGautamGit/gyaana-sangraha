# Sir Arthur I Nightingale
> Red-Haired Human Male Knight bestowed with Knighthood for bravely defending the [[Ivara II Bronze\|Queen's]] life. He also holds the Lordship of [[Owlesia\|Owlesia]] after [[The Raebershire Rebellion\|The Raebershire Rebellion]].

Before Arthur was even born, the Nightingale family had been serving the Forechesters very well. When Arthur became a lord and married into the Drakona [[House White\|House White]], the family became a part of the royals as [[House Nightingale\|House Nightingale]].

### Biographical & Personal Information
- Born: 1967 [[Lurovian Calendar\|SLC]] in the city of [[Triton\|Triton]], [[Owlesia\|Owlesia]]
- Father: [[Edward Nightingale\|Edward Nightingale]] (military general)
- Mother: [[Marilynn Renee\|Marilynn Renee]] (commoner)
- Siblings: [[Nyx I Raebershire\|Nyx I Raebershire]]
- Spouse: [[Nysa I White\|Nysa I White]] (m. 1989 SLC)
- Children: [[Lucian I Nightingale\|Lucian I Nightingale]]
- Affiliations: [[House Nightingale\|House Nightingale]], [[House White\|House White]], [[House Raebershire\|House Raebershire]] (formerly)
- Citizen: [[Kingdom of Dragonpeaks\|Kingdom of Dragonpeaks]]
- Titles Held: [[Triton\|Baron of Triton]] (1980-1985 SLC), [[Owlesia\|Duke of Owlesia]] (since 1985 SLC)
