# Polygamy
> The Social Practice often encouraged by religious codes (such as in [[Islam\|Islam]]) and permissible under regional laws where a person can have more than 1 spouses at the same time.

Polygamy was practiced historically by many mythical and historical royal families that followed [[Hinduism\|Hinduism]] but most of them were political marriages. Ordinary [[Hinduism\|Hindu]] families staunchly believe in [[Monogamy\|Monogamy]].

In religions such as [[Islam\|Islam]] where Polygamy is permitted, it is only permitted to Men while in [[Hinduism\|Hinduism]], Polygamy or [[Monogamy\|Monogamy]] was not a restriction as long as you could feed all your spouses and carry out all other religious affairs fairly easily. Even women were permitted for having multiple husbands in [[Hinduism\|Hinduism]] similar to how many men were allowed to take multiple wives - connotations of being unfaithful were attahed to both cases and only the rich could afford multiple spouses. It is often theorised that polygamy was abandoned by the masses and ater kings due to affiliation with [[Islam\|Islam]]
