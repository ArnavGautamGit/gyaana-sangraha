# Feminism
> An Ideology and Viewpoint which asserts the female point of view of equitable representation in society and began with demanding equal rights.

Feminism began in the 1940s and is often studied in waves since society has rapidly changed with time and the stance of Feminism has also progressed along the same. 

### How many Waves of Feminism are there?
There are many different scholars who model the movement into somewhere between 3 to 5 waves with little to no consensus. Waves are made of individual movements similar to a political wave which may or may not be linear. The main root of the confusion in the number of waves stems from this very reason that the movements counted across different waves may overlap somewhere and there is no exact date for marking the changes of waves apart from a few instances here and there.

I will follow the 4 wave model for the most part unless I feel that Feminism is changing across the 2020s and merits a new wave.
