# Structure of the Indian Constitution
> Note that defines the basic structure of the [[Constitution of India\|Constitution of India]] into Parts & Schedules, where each Part contains many Articles and Schedules contain extra information & context such as defining the major languages of the land (Eighth Schedule)

It is an important topic in [[Indian Polity & Governance\|Indian Polity & Governance]].
Indian Polity is often the subject of many Government Exams like [[UPSC Civil Services Exam\|UPSC CSE]] and [[Combined Graduate Level (CGL) Exam\|SSC CGL]].

### Parts & Schedules
- Part I - Territory & Citizenship
- Part II - Population
- Part III - [[Fundamental Rights\|Fundamental Rights]]
- Part IV - [[Directive Principles of State Policy\|Directive Principles of State Policy]]
