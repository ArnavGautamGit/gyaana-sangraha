# Satoshi (SAT)
> Satoshi is the smallest unit of [[Bitcoin (BTC)\|Bitcoin (BTC)]] named after Satoshi Nakamoto - the only piece of confirmed information about the man who founded of Bitcoin is his name.

$$\Large\text{1 Bitcoin} = \text{100 Million Satoshis}$$
SAT is primarily operated on the [[Lightning Network (LN)\|Lightning Network (LN)]] based [[Cryptocurrency\|Cryptocurrency]].
