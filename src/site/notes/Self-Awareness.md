# Self-Awareness
> An important skill to be aware of yourselves and understand what makes one unique. It helps one understand the reasoning behind their decision-making so that one can reduce the number of decisions one makes in life by already eliminating the options one would never take (because they know themselves now).

 This is a note that I will use both to explain the concept (as I figure it out) and also as an index note which holds a list of notes which describe my personal behaviour and conversational cues. 

### What is Self-Awareness?
It is classically defined as the awareness of the self, on the most basic level it requires you to understand you are a human & inherently different from a rabbit, cat, dog, monkey etc. While on the hogher levels it requires one to realise:
- Who Am I? What are my likes & dislikes?
- What my goals from this life?
- Which paths am I willing to take in order to achieve my goals?
- Which paths am I ***NOT*** willing to take in order to achieve my goals?


### How is Self-Awareness Helpful? How to get better at it?
All I know for now is that Self-Awareness can be enhanced using [[Self-Awareness Techniques\|Self-Awareness Techniques]] to be more mindful about yourselves, your habits, your body and your mind.
