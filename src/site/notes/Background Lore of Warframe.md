# Background Lore of Warframe
> This note talks about Warframe's Background Lore with the prespective of using it to figure out why certain [[Elemental Damage\|Elemental Damage]] is more effective against one than the other.

The Story of Warframe begins with the [[Orokin\|Orokin]], who are a humanoid dynasty of rulers who eventually ruled the Origin System - which is the name of our solar system where Earth, Mars, Venus, Mercury and such planets, moons and dwarf planets exist. The [[Dax\|Dax]] served as the honourable human soldiers of the Orokin Empire. The Dax could be compared to the Samurai who were serving the ruling class.

Orokin needed more soldiers who can fight on the frontlines to avoid the Dax casualties since Dax were highly trained soldiers and it would take years to replace a single death. So the Orokin cloned the living shit out of someone (we have not been explicitly told who it is). The resulting [[Grineer\|Grineer]] became loyal soldiers of the Orokin Empire who fought for the Orokin masters alongside the [[Dax\|Dax]].

Grineer in the Orokin Era were called "Grineer Prime" since over time the cloning would start to dilute the original DNA a lot more by the time the game begins. Grineer & Grineer Primes were born with powerful bones and muscle which allowed them to wear a shit ton of Armour. This is why [[Grineer\|Grineer]] are weak to Corrosive [[Elemental Damage\|Elemental Damage]] since Corrosive Damage & Corrosion in general acts to weaken and negate the strengths of armour. Effectively making it easier to penetrate.

### The Old War
Orokin created the [[Sentients\|Sentients]] so they can spread the Orokin Empire into the new Tau System across the Void on Zariman series ships named as "Zariman 1-2" or "Zariman 1-0". The Zariman ships were the only interstellar travel ships and were also used by the Tenno who are named after the "Zariman 10-0" since the Zero is often pronounced as "Oh" this is how they were named Ten-Oh or Tenno. The players play as the Tenno in the game.

Crossing the Void Wormhole has a weird side-effect on the Sentients and which makes them sterile i.e., they are now unable to give birth. The Sentients were not yet called such but they received the name upon developing Free Will when they reached and began colonising the Tau System.

While the Orokin sent the [[Sentients\|Sentients]] to Tau, they also promised their [[Grineer\|Grineer]] soldiers more land to inhabit and extract resources from since the Origin System was getting quite over-populated due to Orokin not knowing sustainable population increase especially when you are not having births but cloning them - a process where you have full control over things.

Despite engineering and trying to use biological weapons such as the [[Technocyte Virus\|Technocyte Virus]] against the Sentients, the Orokin were not able to win the war. The Old War lasted many millenia and ended in a peace treaty between the [[Sentients\|Sentients]] and the [[Orokin\|Orokin]] which stated that Sentients have sovereign control over the Tau System and Orokin need to leave. The Old Peace is established.

A Quest named the Old Peace explains the dynamics of what happens during the time and how multiple rebellions destroyed the Orokin Empire on the Origin System while the Sentients.

### Quests and Present-Day Lore
When you boot up the game and [[Avoid the Paradox Path\|Avoid the Paradox Path]], you will jump directly into the quest named *Vor's Prize* which will introduce present-day [[Grineer\|Grineer]] and their plan to remote control you using the device named as Ascaris which is the weaker and cruder version of the Orokin Era device of the same name. The original Ascaris Device is now known as the Ascaris Prime similar to other original Warframes and original versions of Orokin-Era Weapons and Warframes.

The Corpus and the situation on Venus is explored by Quests like *The Teacher*, *Vox Solaris*. While the Deadlock Protocol takes place largely on Mercury. Other Side Quests introduce the modern [[Infested\|Infested]] which have mutated and evolved from the [[Technocyte Virus\|Technocyte Virus]].

You explore more of the story as go along in subsequent quests.
