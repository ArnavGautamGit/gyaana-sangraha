# Sri Lanka
> [[State (United Nations)\|UN Member State]] in the Indian Ocean region sharing a maritime border with India just south of Indian State of Tamil Nadu.

Although it was officially ceded to Sri Lanka in 1974, the Tamil Nadu DMK government frequently brings up the [[Kacchatheevu Island Issue\|Kacchatheevu Island Issue]] even though the island has been considered a part of Sri Lanka since 14th centuy except a small time in 17th century. 

### Demography
Most are the people in Sri Lanka are Buddhist but since northern portion of Sri Lanka was often for a pretty long time was under the [[Chola Dynasty\|Chola Dynasty]] and later [[Imperial Chola Dynasty\|Imperial Chola Dynasty]] and now there is a significant population of Tamil-speaking Hindus who are also called Sri Lankan Tamils. 

The Buddhist populations speak the Sinhala language while the Hindus speak Tamil and the Hindu population (Tamils) held major representation in education and government jobs due to better education rates for the community.
