# Bitcoin (BTC)
> Bitcoin (also known as BTC) is the oldest major [[Cryptocurrency\|Cryptocurrency]] based on [[Blockchain\|Blockchain]] Technology. It has rapidly increased in price ever since 2016 going from 1 Bitcoin being worth under $100 USD compared to 1 BTC being worth more than $70,000 USD in 2026.

Believed to have been started by a person under the pseudonym Satoshi Nakamoto in 2009, Bitcoin is one of the few [[Cryptocurrency\|Cryptocurrencies]] that is traded with a second-layer [[Blockchain\|Blockchain]] known as the [[Lightning Network (LN)\|Lightning Network (LN)]] which operates using a smaller unit of Bitcoin known as [[Satoshi (SAT)\|Satoshi (SAT)]] such that 1 SAT = 0.00000001 BTC i.e., 1 BTC = 100,000,000 SATs i.e., 100 Million Sats form 1 Bitcoin.

### Ordinal Numbers
One can mine Bitcoin on either the main chain (the old chain since 2009) or Layer 2 chain also known as the [[Lightning Network (LN)\|Lightning Network (LN)]]. When mined from LN chain, the Satoshis have a unique number reflective of the order in which it was mined, it is called an Ordinal Number.
