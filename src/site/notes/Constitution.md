# Constitution
> The word is developed from "constitute" and means "to frame, establish or compose the relationship between the rulers and the populace - often including the nature of how rulership shall be passed on (via hereditary or elections or both)"

It is an important document that is required by every country to have under International Conventions and to be recognised as a [[State (United Nations)\|UN Member State]]. 

Similarly, India has the [[Constitution of India\|Constitution of India]] drafted on 26th November 1949 and adopted on 26th January 1950.
