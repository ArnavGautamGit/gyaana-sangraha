# Satlantis
> [[NOSTR\|NOSTR]] client that functions as a travel app for users. It is similar to an app where you can check for places to travel and maintain a list of all the places you have traveled before.

All the data in Satlantis is encrypted. No one else can see your data unless they have your Nostr Key Pair of Public & Private Key (the attacker needs both but can only get 1)
