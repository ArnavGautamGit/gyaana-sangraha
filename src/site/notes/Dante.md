# Dante
> Introduced in Whispers in the Walls Quest, Dante is a warframe that essentially resembles a Wizard from the medieval fantasy and high fantasy genres. It uses a mechanic of storing the order of abilities cast ot change the outcome of the 4th ability which is never seen before in any Warframe.

It is peculiar in a way that it has its first ability Noctua which is an Exalted Tome weapon with no limits on Tome Mods - specific mods that are used by Tome Weapons. The only other Tome weapon was the Grimoire which has a defined limit.

The Second Ability is called the "Light Verse" which gives you some Overguard.
The Third Ability is called the "Dark Verse" which deals some Slash Damage.

The Fouth Ability is called the "Final Verse" which amplies the effects of the last two abilities casted among the 2nd and 3rd ability showcased on a Open Book Icon above your ammo on the bottom right of your UI where the two open pages record the last combo of Light & Dark Verses. 

If you use the Light Verse twice before casting the Final Verse you and all allies get a ton of overguard. If you use the Dark Verse twice before casting the Final Verse you nuke the entire area within your line of sight i.e., you will have to turn around to attack behind you by casting Dark Verse twice and then Final Verse again.

### How to Play Dante?
The easiest way to play Dante is to just three major steps depending upon the build.
We first go and get ourselves some massive Overguard with Light Verse, Light Verse and Final Verse (which is your 2nd ability twice and then 4th ability) also called as a 2-2-4.

Then we get ourselves some Pageflight, we go 3-2-4 i.e., Dark, Light and Final Verse.
Pageflight will create some magical pages that will distract enemies by pecking them like birds. They could be used to embue status effects but they are mostly for show.

With overguard, especially with the Noctua build given below, we aim to destroy the enemies with some shit tons of damage by activating the 1st Ability also named Noctua. If the crowd gets too heavy, we go for two dark verses (3rd ability twice) before hitting the Final Verse for nuking the room. It is also called a 3-3-4 ability combination.

We will get to his 1st ability build later but for now I will give you a screenshot of the build you want for the Warframe itself.
![Dante Build.png](/img/user/Vaulted%20Images/Dante%20Build.png)

Archon Vitality is only found by doing Archon Hunts, which are unlocked after you finish the following 2 quests: *The New War* and the *Veilbreaker*. Archon Vitality deals extra heat status apart from adding Health to Dante i.e., if we run heat damage on Noctua then we get great amounts of Heat Status. 
***==Why Heat Damage?==*** Since it does damage overtime and enemies cannot attack you while on fire given you have enough heat status.
