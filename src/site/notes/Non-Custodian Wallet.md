# Non-Custodian Wallet
> It is a type of Digital Wallet used to store [[Cryptocurrency\|Cryptocurrency]] where the keys to that Wallet remain in the hands of the users. It is the opposite of a [[Custodian Wallet\|Custodian Wallet]].

This is great because although you have to manage your keys and seed phrases yourselves, Non-Custodian Wallets cannot be stolen from unless they know your seed phrases (which are used to generate your keys).

The most well-known and well respected name in the business is Metamask for a long time ever since 2021 and 2022...
