# Spotting Fake Friends in Life
> Spotting Fake Frends in Life make you question repeatedly on why are you even friends with a person and make you feel whether you are looking at things too deeply

Here is a guide on how to spot fake friends in 3 easy ways.
1. When you tell them no on a favour that strictly favours them, if they become immediately offended, chances are they are a fake friends because real friends respect your bandwidth.
2. Fake Friends rarely applause, they hide their jealousy as passive-aggressive jokes. Sometimes they celebrate publically but not privately.
3. Are they trying to build an empire with you or leveraging your network to bypass you and build an empire alone?

Betrayal always requires proximity and every joke has an element of truth to it.
