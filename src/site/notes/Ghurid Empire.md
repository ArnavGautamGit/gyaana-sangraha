# Ghurid Empire
> An Empire named after its ruler [[Mohammad Ghori\|Mohammad Ghori]] which originates in the city of Ghor (which I think is in Afghanistan today).

The Empire reached its zenith under Mohammad of Ghor, also known by the name "Ghori". and was situated at the region which is presently across Afghanistan, Pakistan, Eastern Iran and parts of Tajikistan (before annexing into India).

### Arrival to India
After their loss at the hands of [[Prithviraja III Chauhan\|Prithviraja III Chauhan]] in [[First Battle of Tarain (1191)\|First Battle of Tarain (1191)]], Ghori used deceit and specific technological advances that used materials prohibited in Hinduism (sourced from cows, buffaloes and bulls) to attack India in [[Second Battle of Tarain (1192)\|Second Battle of Tarain (1192)]] which he won. Partly also due to the political divisions between the [[Rajputana Confederacy\|Rajputana Confederacy]] at the time.

### Setting up the Sultanate
When [[Mohammad Ghori\|Mohammad Ghori]] tried to make Prithviraj his vassal, and Prithviraj refused, Ghori killed him and reluctantly had to set up his [[Mamluk\|Mamluk]] named [[Qutub ud-Din Aibak\|Qutub ud-Din Aibak]] as his general/vassal who [[Delhi Sultanate\|ruled from Delhi as a Sultan]] under Ghori.

It is unclear whether Ghori ordered it or Aibak, but one of the two ordered the general [[Bakhtiyar Khilji\|Bakhtiyar Khilji]] to capture the regions upto Bengal, it was here in the year 1204 that the idiot Bakhtiyar destroyed the [[Nalanda University\|Nalanda University]] and set it on fire instead of learning anything from that library about statecraft. If they just targeted military installations and left the people alone, the people over the next few generations would not have hated these degenerates as much as they do now.

### Satisfying Death (कुत्ते की मौत)
When Ghori FINALLY died like a dog (that he was) in a successful assasination attempt in 1206, his empire slowly collapsed but at the same time, as soon as [[Qutub ud-Din Aibak\|Qutub ud-Din Aibak]] got the news of Ghori's death, he declared independence from the Ghurids as his former master's empire slowly collapsed due to no one to inherit the impotent dickhead's throne leading to his [[Mamluk\|Mamluk]] sultans like Qutub and others declaring independence. Key installations in Eastern Iran like the city of Ghazni, Herat etc were overrun and taken by the [[Shahs of Khwarazm\|Shahs of Khwarazm]] and the cursed empire disappeared from the face of the earth by 1215.
