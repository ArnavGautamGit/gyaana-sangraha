# Smallpox Vaccine
> The world's first vaccine by [[Edward Jenner\|Edward Jenner]] made in 1749 against the disease of [[Smallpox\|Smallpox]]. Before this vaccine was made, people used the Cowpox Virus to protect themselves from Smallpox.

This was a live virus vaccine and the first of its kind in 1749. One of the radical ideas which proved that [[Radicalism is the Mother of Societal Change\|Radicalism is the Mother of Societal Change]].
