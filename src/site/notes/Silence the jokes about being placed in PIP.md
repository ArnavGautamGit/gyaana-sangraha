# Silence the jokes about being placed in PIP
> Being placed in the [[Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)\|Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)]] is a bad enough situation but if people - even if they are your managers - are cracking JOKES about them, you need to silence them once and for all.

This guide brings you back your dignity in case of active jokes being cracked on your PIP status by anyone since Public Humiliation at the office is a form of Workplace Harrassment that should not be ignored as a part of a "joke". 

### Step 1: Do not let them break you in that room!
As always in all of my notes regarding [[Office Politics & How to Handle it\|Office Politics & How to Handle it]], I have stated time and again to not fall for your emotions, stay logical and cold. Stare straight through them and continue working as if nothing happened.

### Step 2: Bypass them completely
The moment you are done being in that room you leave and start typing an email which will go straight to HR:

> [!cite] They may have won the battle, You will win the war
> "My Confidential PIP status was weaponised creating an openly toxic work environment where people are laughed on when they falter instead of helping them back up."

You are no longer an underperforming employee, you are a legal liability who can slap a workplace harrassment case even after being fired (firing you will make matters worse).

### Step 3: Protecting Yourself
You take radical accountability for your exit strategy and always remember that a boss who does this bullshit will never ever in a million years promote you even if Elon Musk vouches for you and campaigns for you to become the next [[President of the United States (POTUS)\|President of the United States (POTUS)]].

You go ahead and survive the PIP, you spend that extra energy into applying for new jobs. Stay Mentally Strong and control only what you can control.
