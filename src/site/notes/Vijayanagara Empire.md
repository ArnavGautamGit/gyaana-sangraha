# Vijayanagara Empire
> An empire based in Deccan India which controlled much of the southern Peninsular India which includes parts of present-day Kerala, Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Goa and Maharashtra apart from controlling the entirety of Karnataka. All ruled from the capital named "Vijayanagara" (transl. "Victory City" or "City of Victory")

Vijayanagara Empire with its capital at Vijayanagara (in present-day Karnataka) was also referred to as the Karnata Kingdom in certain local inscriptions. The Portuguese and other European powers referred to it as "The Kingdom of Narasinga" where Narasinga is derived from the Hindu God [[Lord Narasimha\|Lord Narasimha]].

Although the Empire ruled till 1703 [[Vikram Samvat Calendar\|VS]] (i.e., 1646 AD), it was greatly weakened after fighting the combined armies of 5 Deccan Sultanates at [[Battle of Talikota (1565)\|Battle of Talikota]] in 1622 [[Vikram Samvat Calendar\|VS]] (i.e., 1565 AD). It is also called the Battle of Kannighāti or the Battle of Rakshasi-Tangdi.

### Ruling Dynasties
There were four major dynasties that ruled the Vijayanagara Empire at different times of their history: (all dates are according to [[Vikram Samvat Calendar\|Vikram Samvat Calendar]] with [[Gregorian Calendar\|Gregorian]] dates in brackets)
1. [[Karnata Sangama Dynasty\|Karnata Sangama Dynasty]] from 1393 to 1537 (i.e., 1336 AD to 1485 AD)
2. [[Saluva Dynasty\|Saluva Dynasty]] from 1537 to 1543 (i.e., 1485 AD to 1491 AD)
	1. [[First Era of Dual Karnata Kings\|First Era of Dual Karnata Kings]] from 1543 to 1557 (i.e., 1491 AD to 1505 AD)
		1. A period of regent emperors and shift of dynasty from Saluva to Tuluva
3. [[Tuluva Dynasty\|Tuluva Dynasty]] from 1543 to 1599 (i.e., 1491 AD to 1542 AD)
	1. [[Second Era of Dual Karnata Kings\|Second Era of Dual Karnata Kings]] from 1599 to 1627 (1542 AD to 1570 AD)
		1. A period of regent emperors and shift of dynasty from Tuluva to Aravidu
5. [[Aravidu Dynasty\|Aravidu Dynasty]] from 1599 to 1703 (1542 AD to 1646 AD)

### Cultural Contributions
Literally began a new form of music called "[[Carnatic Music\|Carnatic Music]]" and revived the Hindu forms of music by patronising them instead of the muslim [[Sufism\|Sufism]] and [[Qawwali\|Qawwali]] bullshit. Among music they brought music back.

The Vijayanagara Empire was single-handely responsible for bringing back the historic practice of [[Indian Mural Paintings\|Indian Mural Paintings]] that had been going on since the times of the great [[Sātavāhana Empire\|Sātavāhana Empire]] and [[Mauryan Empire\|Mauryan Empire]]. They did not promote many [[Indian Miniature Paintings\|Miniature Paintings]] due to them being introduced to India by Islamic and Iranic artists who arrived in India with the [[Islamisation of Sindh\|Islamisation of Sindh]] and [[Delhi Sultanate\|Delhi Sultanate]].

Vijayanagara Empire was based on pure Hindu Culture and they promoted & patronised the same. They also patronised the [[Performing Arts\|Performing Arts]] such as [[Indian Classical Theatre (Nātya)\|Indian Classical Theatre (Nātya)]] and many [[Indian Classical Dance Styles\|Indian Classical Dance Styles]] including starting their own (just like in Music).
