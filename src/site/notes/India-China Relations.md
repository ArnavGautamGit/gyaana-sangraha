# India-China Relations
> Bilateral Relations that two major [[State (United Nations)\|UN Member States]] of [[India\|India]] and [[China\|China]] share - especially as the two are economic powerhouses sharing a border.

Countires like [[Nepal\|Nepal]], [[Myanmar\|Myanmar]] and [[Bhutan\|Bhutan]] often complain on being in the crossfire between the two nations.

China aims to restrict India using the [[String of Pearls Strategy\|String of Pearls Strategy]] while India is responding with the [[Necklace of Diamonds Strategy\|Necklace of Diamonds Strategy]].
