# Deal With Workplace Gossip
> Gossip at the workplace is nothing new, it is just that negative rumours about personal life or targeting gossips are categorised as Workplace Harrassment and must be dealt with accordingly.

Did someone in the office gossip or start a rumour about YOU so colleagues are passing by your table smiling at each other when they see you? Follow this guide!

## Level 1: Do NOT react Immediately
Immediate reactions driven by emotions will be seen as crashouts. Captain Crashout loses their job. You need to stay completely unbothered while you gather the facts and Document everything as discussed in [[Office Politics & How to Handle it\|Office Politics & How to Handle it]].

Here is all that you need to gather:
1. Who heard it?
2. Who did they hear it from?
3. Exactly what was said about you.

### Level 2: The Leadership Trap
You pull the root person that spread the gossip/rumour into a 1-on-1 meeting and play the student - they should not find out that you know it is them who started the rumour.

> [!cite] Rough Idea for what to say in the conversation
> "There is a really damaging rumour going around here and since you are someone I know as wise enough to deal with this (if they are your boss/manager say they are someone you look up to instead) How do you suggest we deal with this situation and with whoever that is spreading this rumour?"

Now watch them sweat their forehead off as they try to give you advice on how to destroy themselves but listen closely, they might actually leave a tip here and there.

### Level 3: The Liability Drop
You follow up the meeting with an email that says this:

> [!cite] The "Thank You" Email
> "Thanks for our discussion about the false claims being spread about me. Let me know what our next steps are to resolve the situation"

Why are you thanking them? Because you were successfully able to turn the gossip into a documented HR Liability that incriminates the company for Worplace Harrassment without them knowing about it.

Since you have required evidence in Level 1, at the drop of a dime, at any time of your choosing, you can now EASILY file a lawsuit of workplace harrassment supplemented with this email trail of the person accepting the problem and giving you tips & advice to incriminate the entire company and even get the other person potentially fired along with a big sum of money as settlement.
