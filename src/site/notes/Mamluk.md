# Mamluk
> a term of Persian/Arabic origin that was used widely in the Muslim world for a non-arab slaves who serve as soldiers and may or may not have been freed by their master.

Many dynasties were spanned by these Mamluks with two of them named "Mamluk Dynasty" because you tend to lose braincells with the inbreeding the muslims loved (and SOME like those in Pakistan, Bangladesh and even UK still do).

Mamluk Dynasties include: [[Ghaznavid Empire\|Ghaznavid Empire]], [[Delhi Sultanate\|Delhi Sultanate]], [[Mamluk Sultanate (Egypt)\|Mamluk Sultanate (Egypt)]] among others I will add here soon enough.
