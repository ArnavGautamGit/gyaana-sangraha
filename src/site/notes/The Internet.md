# The Internet
> It is a worldwide [[Computer Networks\|Network]] of various [[Computer Networks\|Computer Networks]]. You could understand it as the network of networks.

Before the Internet, Technology was limited to conversing with each other if it was physically placed at a small enough gap, the [[Telegraph Machine\|Telegraph Machine]] changed it substantially. It allowed communication at a blazing-fast speed. [[Telephones\|Telephones]] made it possible to transmit audio as well and actually hear the other person. 

### How can a Computer be Connected to a Network?
The way a singular network is made by connecting various computers together by either [[Telephone Cables\|Telephone Cables]]. Faster Cables called [[Ethernet Cables\|Ethernet Cables]] were made with advancements in technology. 

Later multiple computers could be connected in a network wirelessly using [[Routers\|Routers]]. Later even in wired connections, specific [[Ethernet Cables\|Ethernet Cables]] made the connections faster, now we use [[Fibre Optic Cables\|Fibre Optic Cables]] to connect via wire. 

### Basic Concept behind the Internet
All you have to do to connect multiple networks is to connect one group of computers via the use of [[Ethernet Cables\|Ethernet Cables]] or a [[Routers\|Router]] to form a [[Local Area Network (LAN)\|Local Area Network (LAN)]]. If you connect one LAN in one region with another LAN in some other region via either [[Fibre Optic Cables\|Fibre Optic Cables]] or Wireless Connections, you form a network that contains two networks. 

This process repeated over the entire world creates a network where any computer from a particular part of the world can connect to any other computer in another part of the world.
