# India
> [[State (United Nations)\|UN Member State]] in Asia to the north of Indian Ocean. I am an Indian Citizen and I am obviously biased towards Indian Interest & Hindu Interests.

India shares Land border with [[Pakistan\|Pakistan]], [[China\|China]], [[Nepal\|Nepal]], [[Bhutan\|Bhutan]], [[Bangladesh\|Bangladesh]] and [[Myanmar\|Myanmar]] from West to East. Meanwhile India shares a maritime border with [[Sri Lanka\|Sri Lanka]] in the south and through [[Andaman & Nicobar Islands\|Andaman & Nicobar Islands]] we hold a maritime border with [[Thailand\|Thailand]] and [[Malaysia\|Malaysia]].
