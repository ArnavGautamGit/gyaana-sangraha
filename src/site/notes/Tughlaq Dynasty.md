# Tughlaq Dynasty
> The Third Dynasty to rule from the throne of the [[Delhi Sultanate\|Delhi Sultanate]] and arguably the time when it reaches its Zenith and where it collapses.

This Dynasty was somehow up there with one of the top 5 most cruelest of rulers directly followed up with the top 5 most clueless of leaders. I swear it must be magic...
Not that I care anyway... one less empire of degenerates.

### Sequence of Sultāns
15. [[Ghiyasuddin Tughlaq\|Ghiyasuddin Tughlaq]] ---> 1320 AD to 1326 AD
16. [[Mohammad bin Tughlaq\|Mohammad bin Tughlaq]] ---> 1326 AD to 1351 AD
17. [[Feroz Shah Tughlaq\|Feroz Shah Tughlaq]] ---> 1351 AD to 1388 AD
18. [[Mahmoud Tughlaq\|Mahmoud Tughlaq]] ---> 1388 AD to 1412 AD

### Decline
The Tughlaqs became weak after the Death of Ghiyasuddin and their weaknesses were overlooked initially by throwing Mohammad bin Tughlaq under the bus by saying it was just 1 bad ruler & they would be right if the same problems were not similarly more visible in Feroz Shah's time.

Feroz/Firuz was in a situation where he was busy his entire reign on just trying to prevent the kingdom from disintegrating & built a few canals on the side to gain public support while his taxation policy (especially with respect to [[Jizya\|Jizya]]) was to get support from the religious Ullema among other nobles.

In Mahmoud's Era the dynasty was exposed multiple times where [[Khizra Khan\|Khizra Khan]], the general of [[Timur\|Timur]] invaded Delhi & the throne was empty for almost 2 full years. Most of the empire broke away & announced independence from the Sultanate. They even started issuing their own coins. It was here when [[Khizra Khan\|Khizra Khan]] established the [[Sayyid Dynasty\|Sayyid Dynasty]] which was not able to accomplish jack shit across its 4 generations of rulers - only to be replaced by [[Behlol Lodhi\|Behlol Lodhi]] who established the [[Lodhi Dynasty\|Lodhi Dynasty]] (this guy wasn't able to do much either).

First documented use of Gunpowder in [[Early Firearms\|Early Firearms]] and [[Cannon Artillery\|Cannon Artillery]] in India brought by [[Babur\|Babur]] in the [[First Battle of Panipat (1526)\|First Battle of Panipat (1526)]] worked for more or less a simple victory for him to establish the [[Mughal Empire\|Mughal Empire]] in India & end the [[Delhi Sultanate\|Delhi Sultanate]] once & for all.
