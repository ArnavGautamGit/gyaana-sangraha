# Gulchehra Begum
> Daughter of [[Babur\|Babur]] and blood-sister of [[Gulbadan Begum\|Gulbadan Begum]].
> She travelled to India from Iran in 1557 AD but due to being much elder than Gulbadan she could not survive the journey in the heat and passed away later in the same year.

Being the daughter of Babur, she was the half-sister of [[Humayun\|Humayun]], [[Kamran Mirza\|Kamran]] and [[Mohammad Askari Mirza\|Askari]].
When she died in 1557, she was 41 years old.
