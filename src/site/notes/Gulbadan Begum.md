# Gulbadan Begum
> The second daughter of [[Babur\|Babur]] who came to India in 1557 with [[Gulchehra Begum\|Gulchehra Begum]] who did not survive the journey due to age. She later wrote the [[Humāyūn Nāmā\|Humāyūn Nāmā]] for [[Humayun\|Humayun]] when he became the new emperor. 

Being the Daughter of [[Babur\|Babur]], it means she was the half-sister of [[Humayun\|Humayun]], [[Kamran Mirza\|Kamran]], [[Mohammad Askari Mirza\|Askari]] and full-sister of [[Gulchehra Begum\|Gulchehra Begum]] (due to being born from the same mother).
