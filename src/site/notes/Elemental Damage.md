# Elemental Damage
> This is a damage guide for [[Warframe\|Warframe]]. This guide helps players optimise their damage in Warframe despite the live-service, ever-changing nature of Warframe.

Elemental Damage refers to using Elemental Mods that rely on the Status chance percentage to add the element's effects to those elements onto the target hit unless the target is inanimate since then you will not see changes.

### Why Elemental Damage is Superior to IPS Damage?
Elemental Damage is considered superior to Impact, Puncture, Slash damage (called IPS in short) because IPS mods only take the damage dealt by their own type and add to that while an Elemental Damage Mod will take the sum of all base damage types (I+P+S) to add elemental damage on top of that.

For Example: Say that a weapon does 80 damage divided as 30 impact, 40 puncture, 10 slash damage. For such a weapon, a mod that adds +30% slash only adds 3 damage to make the total damage 83. If you add a mod that adds +30% heat then the 30% of 80 will be added to the weapon i.e., the total damage will be 104. The jump from 80 to 104 is 8 times the jump from 80 to 83.
