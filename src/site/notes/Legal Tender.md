# Legal Tender
> Commodities accepted and backed by the government of any [[State (United Nations)\|UN Member State]] as a Legal Medium of Exchange.

Although most states only accept [[Fiat Money\|Fiat Money]] along with their respective [[Digital Financial Transactions\|Digital Transaction]] Currencies like [[Central Bank Digital Currency (CBDC)\|CBDC]], some countries are seen as accepting particular or popular [[Cryptocurrency\|Cryptocurrency]] like [[Bitcoin (BTC)\|Bitcoin (BTC)]] as Legal Tender.
