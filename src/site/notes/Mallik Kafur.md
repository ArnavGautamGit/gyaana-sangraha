# Mallik Kafur
> [[Mamluk\|Mamluk]] slave of [[Alauddin Khilji\|Alauddin Khilji]] who was loyal to the very end. Ruled the [[Delhi Sultanate\|Delhi Sultanate]] for 1 month as the Regent (Nāib Sultān) when Alauddin's younger son [[Shihab ud-Din Omar\|Shihab ud-Din Omar]] was the actual Sultān (but he was a toddler) 

![[Mallik Kafur.png\|Mallik Kafur.png]]

He partook in many of the invasions of the Sultanate into the Hindu kingdoms and hence he cannot be considered innocent in the eyes of Hindus ever. Even if he was doing things against his wish, it was not the right thing to do.

### Life with Khilji & Possibilities of being Gay
His life and his story is addressed in a dedicated subheading for him in the original note for [[Alauddin Khilji\|Alauddin Khilji]]. Kafur was very shrewd and it is said that he might have been the guilty gay pleasure of Khilji himself. Which is why in popular culture like Padmaavat, Kafur is shown as a guy who behaves like a gay male trying to hide the fact he is gay.
