# Monogamy
> A social practice often enforced by religious codes and regional laws which mandate to only be married to one spouse at a time.

If a person divorces their existing spouse and marries another, they are also considered monogamous.

The Social Practice of having more than 1 spouse at the same is called [[Polygamy\|Polygamy]].
Although [[Polygamy\|Polygamy]] is not followed by the masses, it was practiced by practicing [[Hinduism\|Hindu]] kings and royal families across India. Later, Polygamy was affiliated with [[Islam\|Islam]].
