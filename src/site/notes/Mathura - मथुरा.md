# Mathura - मथुरा
> One of the most prominent cities in [[Indian Art & Culture\|Indian Culture]] and [[Hinduism\|Hinduism]] which contributed the most in shaping [[Indic Philosophy\|Indic Philosophy]].

Mathura was also linked with [[Lord Krishna\|Lord Krishna]] since this is where he killed Kansa/Kamsa (कंस).
At the time of [[Ghaznavid Raid of Mathura\|Ghaznavid Raid of Mathura]], it was the richest city in the world.

Mathura has always been a home of art, sculpture and temples across various Empires in [[Indian History\|Indian History]] and has also been one of the [[Eternal Cities of India\|Eternal Cities of India]].

Mathura has played an important role in Indian History and Culture ever since the days of the [[Kushan Empire\|Kushan Empire]].
