# Mubarak Shah Khilji
> The elder son of [[Alauddin Khilji\|Alauddin Khilji]] and the 14th [[Delhi Sultanate\|Sultān of Delhi]] after displacing his brother [[Shihab ud-Din Omar\|Shihab ud-Din Omar]] while he (Omar) was a toddler with [[Mallik Kafur\|Mallik Kafur]] ruling as Omar's regent.

![[Mubarak Shah Khilji.png\|Mubarak Shah Khilji.png]]

He reigned as the Sultān for approximately 4 years and was regarded as a weak king since his territories kept dwindling with him unable to hold them together. Due to being a weak king, he lost to [[Ghiyasuddin Tughlaq\|Ghazi Malik]] which ushered the era of the [[Tughlaq Dynasty\|Tughlaq Dynasty]] as Ghazi Malik took the regnal name "[[Ghiyasuddin Tughlaq\|Ghiyasuddin Tughlaq]]".
