# Restrained (DH)
> A condition in [[Compendiums/Daggerheart Compendium/Daggerheart\|Daggerheart]] where a creature cannot move. The creature is free to make rolls to free themselves but the rolls won't necessarily succeed. If they do suceed, previously restrained creature is free.

The Creature has diadvantage on rolls against other creatures, but other creatures have advantage on rolls against them.

Note: A Restrained Creature may or may not be [[Vulnerable (DH Condition)\|Vulnerable]] or [[Hidden (DH Condition)\|Hidden]].
