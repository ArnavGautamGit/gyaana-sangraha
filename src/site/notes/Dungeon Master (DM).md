# Dungeon Master (DM)
> A person who hosts a [[Compendiums/D&D Compendium/Dungeons & Dragons\|Dungeons & Dragons]] game for a group of players.
> It is a trademarked term which is the [[Compendiums/D&D Compendium/Dungeons & Dragons\|D&D]]-specific term for [[Game Master (GM)\|Game Master (GM)]]

Dungeon Master is responsible for planning encounters and the story and facilitating the said story in the form of a campaign explored over a multiple gaming sessions.
