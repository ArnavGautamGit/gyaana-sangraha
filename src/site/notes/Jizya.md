# Jizya
> An Extra Tax charged by an Islamic regime from non-muslims. Muslims do not have to pay the Jizya. Rates of the Jizya change across jurisdictions and time periods but 

Most prominently demanded in [[Mughal Empire\|Mughal Empire]] and [[Delhi Sultanate\|Delhi Sultanate]] before the Mughals, it was a way to financially subjugate the non-muslims and force the conversion of the poor strata towards Islam. Once the poor moved towards Islam, they can follow the classic muslim playbook of radicalising the newly converted to genocide/expel the non-muslims. It was seen as recently as the [[Kashmiri Hindu Genocide (1990)\|Kashmiri Hindu Genocide (1990)]], [[Bangladeshi Regime Change (2024)\|Bangladeshi Regime Change (2024)]] and the [[Murshidabad Hindu Exodus (2025)\|Murshidabad Hindu Exodus (2025)]].
