# Avoid the Paradox Path
> At the very start of [[Warframe\|Warframe]], you get the choice of starting and following the conventional path or start with the Paradox Path. The conventional path may also be referred to as the Warframe Path.

Paradox Path will confuse you about the story and will retrospectively spoil the following story by a big margin. At the same time, some warframes are easier to get if you go through the Paradox Path but they aren't all that important or so great that you love them.

I would suggest that Paradox Path is not worth the hassle since even after confusing the shit out of yourself about what is going on you would being thrown into the same place in the story as those players who took the Warframe Path - which will make you feel lost since the game mode in the Paradox Path is truly paradoxical to the rest of the game and will make you feel as if anything you did in the other mode was useless.

### What after choosing Conventional?
Going into the conventional path, choose your starter warframe among [[Excalibur\|Excalibur]], [[Volt\|Volt]] and [[Mag\|Mag]].
I would suggest that if you have friends to play with and do not mind a good long-term investment then you can go for Volt, if you want to make the game easier with big damage and helpful mechanics then you can go for Excalibur. I have no experience with Mag so i don't know but all players either take Excalibur or Volt as their starters. Volt is a bigger challenge while Excalibur is easier to play and more beginner friendly.
