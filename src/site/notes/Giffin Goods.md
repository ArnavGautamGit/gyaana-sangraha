# Giffin Goods
> Specific Goods whose prices (even if increased) does not reduce the [[Demand\|Demand]]. They are also called "Inferior Goods" because they might be necessary goods like staple foods. 

Giffin Goods do not follow the [[Demand\|Law of Demand]].
These goods are also called as "Necessary Goods" and "Staple Goods" for example: food, clothes and housing.
