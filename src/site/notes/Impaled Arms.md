# Impaled Arms
> An [[Heraldic Achievement\|Armorial Achievement]] where the Arms of two people are kept on the same shield often such that the arms meet *in pale* (vertical line down the center) with each half having one of the two arms.

Impaled Arms are used to signify marriage and are often used as "Marriage Arms" used by the couple together such as when sending letter invites to other families for their marriage ceremony.
