# Sayyid Dynasty
> The fourth dynasty to rule the [[Delhi Sultanate\|Delhi Sultanate]] after the fall of [[Tughlaq Dynasty\|Tughlaq Dynasty]]. It was one of the least influencial dynasties despite having 4 Sultāns (just like the [[Tughlaq Dynasty\|Tughlaqs]] and the [[Khilji Dynasty\|Khiljis]] before them).

Sayyid Dynasty took over after the raids of [[Timurid Empire\|Timurid Empire]] of [[Timur\|Timur]] displaced the previous Sultān [[Mahmoud Tughlaq\|Mahmoud Tughlaq]] from his throne by the general of Timur, [[Khizra Khan\|Khizra Khan]]. 

While the title of "Sultān" referred to a title which signified ruling in the name of Islam's Caliph or Khalifa in the [[Ummayyad Caliphate\|Ummayyad Caliphate]] or any other Arabian/Turkic Caliphates of the time, Khizra wanted to rule as the "Rayat-i-aalah" which translates to "Subject-in-chief" to signify that [[Timur\|Timur]] is the real ruler & Khizra is his mere subject.

> [!tip] Rayat has many meanings in Arabic & Persian
> Other meanings of Rayat include banner or flag among other words like cultivator/farmer. It is important to note the context before making a generalisation or theory...

### Sequence of Sultans
19. [[Khizra Khan\|Khizra Khan]] (1st "Rayyat-i-Aalah") ---> 1414 AD to 1421 AD
20. [[Mubarak Shah II Sayyid\|Mubarak Shah II Sayyid]] ---> 1421 AD to 1434 AD
21. [[Mohammad Shah\|Mohammad Shah]] ---> 1434 AD to 1443 AD
22. [[Alauddin Alam Shah\|Alauddin Alam Shah]] ---> 1443 AD to 1451 AD

### Disintegration & Transfer of Power to Lodhis
Due to the Tughlaqs already setting the pace for disintegration due to their piss-poor management of a kingdom as large as the one they inherited, when they finally got defeated, all other kingdoms except the region under Timurid control decided to declare Independence.

[[Bengal Sultanate\|Bengal Sultanate]] declared Independence, so did the multiple Rajput Kingdoms and other Central, Western, Eastern and Deccan Indian kingdoms.

The Delhi Sultanate was left with just the small region around parts of contemporary Punjab, Haryana, Delhi and Western Uttar Pradesh.

The subsequent rulers after Khizra Khan are such massive cowards and sissies that their last ruler [[Alauddin Alam Shah\|Alauddin Alam Shah]] even emptied his throne and surrendered his title to [[Bahlol Lodhi\|Bahlol Lodhi]] without war in 1451 as the Timurid Empire backing them itself started to weaken! 

Bahlol Lodhi's [[Lodhi Dynasty\|Lodhi Dynasty]] would end up being the last dynasty of Delhi Sultanate entirely.
