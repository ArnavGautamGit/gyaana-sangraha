# Pulakeshin II
> Most influencial militaristic ruler from the [[Chalukya Dynasty\|Chalukya Dynasty]] which started split into two branches after his death.

Near Vatapi, the capital of the [[Chalukya Dynasty\|Chalukya Dynasty]], the Aihole Inscription carved by Ravikirti mentions how not only Pulakeshin II win against the Pallavas by killing [[Mahendravarman I\|Mahendravarman I]], but Pulakeshin was also able to hold back King [[Harshavardhan\|Harshavardhan]] of the [[Pushyabhuti Dynasty\|Pushyabhuti Dynasty]] at the banks of the Yamuna although Harsh did not die in this conflict.
